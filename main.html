Care. Connection. Comfort.

Solace is a comprehensive platform for hospice and palliative care that coordinates care teams, simplifies medication tracking, and helps families preserve precious memories—all while ensuring dignity and peace of mind.

Everything Families Need,
In One Place

Solace removes the administrative burden from end-of-life care, allowing families to focus on what truly matters.

Care Journal

Track medications, meals, and daily care activities in one simple timeline. No more messy bedside notebooks or forgotten tasks.

Care Team Connection

Direct access to hospice nurses, doctors, and social workers with profiles, messaging, and one-tap emergency calls."

Legacy Vault

Preserve precious memories with video messages, voice notes, letters, and family recipes. Create lasting legacies for generations to come.

Family Circle

Share updates with extended family in one secure place. Stop repeating the same update to 20 different relatives.

Documents & ID

Store DNR orders, living wills, and insurance information securely. Find critical documents in seconds, not hours.

24/7 Support

One-tap access to the hospice nurse on-call line. Help is always available when you need it most.

Focus on Connection, Not Coordination

Solace acts as a gentle, invisible hand that handles the complex logistics of palliative care, freeing families to focus on comfort, dignity, and precious moments together.

  • Reduce caregiver anxiety with organized care tracking
  • Seamless communication between family and care teams
  • Preserve memories that matter most
  • Peace of mind with 24/7 access to support
